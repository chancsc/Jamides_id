Arhopala lurida is a rare member of the epimuta group. It is a smaller relative of Arhopala atosia and is distinguished by its darker underside markings and specific wing measurements.

Underside Identification Keys

Size: A medium-sized species with a forewing length of approximately 19.0–21.0 mm.
